This template is for tracking individual player skills, strengths, weaknesses, and progress on Critical Fitness Variables (CFVs). Ratings (e.g., 1-Needs Work, 2-Developing, 3-Proficient, 4-Excellent) can be applied. Include specific comments for actionable feedback.

Rating Scale Guide:

1 = Needs Significant Improvement: Area requires immediate and focused attention; performance is well below expected level for this stage.
2 = Developing, Inconsistent: Shows potential but lacks consistency; foundational elements may be missing or not yet habitual.
3 = Proficient, Consistent: Demonstrates good understanding and execution; performs reliably at the expected level for this stage.
4 = Excellent, Exceeds Expectations: Displays advanced skill or understanding; consistently performs above the expected level and may show mastery.
